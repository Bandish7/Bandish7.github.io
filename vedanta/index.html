Message from Chairman

“A Warm Welcome to Vedanta School Dear Parents, Students, and Guardians, I am delighted to extend a warm welcome to each of you as the chairman of Vedanta School. It is an honour and privilege to serve in this capacity and be a part of a dedicated team that is committed.”

Madan Kumar Thapa is a musician currently pursing PhD in Music from Tribhuwan Universtiy. He is a music teacher at Nepal Music School, Maiti Nepal, Olympia World School, VS Niketan and so on. He started Vedanta School on 2017 with the name Learning Hands pre-school later on changed to Vedanata serving thousands of kids during their development stage of life.
